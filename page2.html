Food
Korean people's main food is rice and  side dishes. Sometimes they eat street food. They have rice cake on stick, spicy rice cake  and noodles. On Korean New Year, they eat Korean roll, rice cake, dumpling, noodles and seaweed soup. On Christmas, some  Korean people eat seaweed soup, meat and rice because they celebrate  birthdays. There are different kind of chopsticks. In restaurants they have wooden chopsticks, but some people use metal.  Korean people make their own type of food. Korean people make their food for example they make rice  with all different kind of rices are mixed together. Korean people eat the same food as us but they use cabbage such as Korean spicy cabbage. They use Kimchi as a side dish. Kimchi is Korea's national dish.

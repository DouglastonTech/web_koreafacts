Clothing
Korean clothing is different. It is different because their fabric is a little stronger. There is different types of  Korean clothes. They are traditional  clothing. Korean people wear traditional clothing on special days. It is pronounced hanbook. It looks like a dress, but it is a dress that comes with a shirt. If you want to wear it, there might be a little white dress so you put that on first. Then you put on the long dress on and tie the string back then forward . Then you need to put on the shirt . Koreans wear those dresses on New Year's and  parades.
